About the collection

The Suetables Signature Line

Rooted in storytelling and handcrafted in Montreal, the Suetables Signature line is a collection of timeless, customizable jewelry inspired by vintage and old-world design.

Every design is hand-sketched, carved in wax, cast in real gold or silver, and finished with genuine stones and recycled metals. Small treasures, made for connection.

Universal production tips

How to shoot

Shoot vertical (9:16) — optimised for Reels and Stories on Meta

Handheld, slightly imperfect — feels authentic. Trust beats polish at this price tier

Sue wears the piece throughout — product visible from second one

Close-up on charm 3–4 seconds — let the craftsmanship speak

No music needed — voice-only builds more trust at this price tier

Captions always on — 85% of Meta ads are watched without sound

The scripts

Three angles. One collection.
Running simultaneously.

All three videos run simultaneously as Meta ads. Each speaks to a different audience mindset — no overlap, no ad fatigue.

CTA16–20s

Director's note Cold audiences. Leads with Sue's vulnerability. Scarcity lands as a natural consequence of craft — never as pressure. Film in-store or at home in warm natural light.

Shot-by-shot guide

Apply to any script

Shot Timing Beat Direction
1 0–2s Hook Sue looks directly to camera. No intro. Start mid-sentence if possible. Eye contact = scroll stop.
2 2–10s Personal truth Maintain talking head. Frame tight — chest up. Slight lean forward. Warm light on face.
3 10–14s Product reveal Cut to extreme close-up of charm in warm light. Show texture, engraving, stone. Then back to Sue.
4 14–20s CTA Sue finishes holding the piece visibly. Final 1–2s: hold on product against linen or stone surface.
